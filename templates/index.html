Welcome to Eden M-Kopa

Eden M-Kopa is a comprehensive device financing and management platform that enables businesses to offer pay-as-you-go devices with remote lock/unlock capabilities. Built with blockchain integration for secure payment tracking and Android Device Policy Controller for device management.

Key Features

  • 📱 Remote device lock/unlock management
  • 💰 Flexible payment plans and tracking
  • 🔐 Blockchain-based payment verification
  • 📊 Real-time analytics and reporting
  • 👥 Customer and device management
  • 🔔 Automated payment reminders
