1. **Admin** enrolls device with phone number only (no PIN field)
2. **Customer** downloads Eden app v1.9.1

4. **System** checks enrollment and shows registration screen

6. **Device** activates immediately

## 📞 **Support Preparation**

### **Common Customer Questions:**
**Q: Why do I need to re-register?**
A: We upgraded for better security. You now control your own PIN instead of using a default one.

**Q: Will my loan information be lost?**
A: No, all loan details are preserved. Only the PIN is reset for security.

**Q: What if I can't remember my phone number format?**
A: Use the same number you gave during enrollment. The app accepts various formats.

**Q: How long do I have to register?**
A: Please complete within 7 days to avoid any service interruption.
